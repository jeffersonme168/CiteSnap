## 详细描述（英文）

### CiteSnap - Extract AI Search Citations in One Click

CiteSnap helps you instantly extract search keywords and citation links from AI search engine responses — no more manual copying one URL at a time.

**What it does:**
When AI search engines (like Doubao, DeepSeek, Perplexity, Kimi, Metaso) answer your questions, they search the web and cite multiple sources. CiteSnap captures all of this data in one click and lets you export it as structured data.

**Key Features:**
• One-click extraction of search keywords and citation URLs
• Multi-turn conversation support — correctly pairs each question with its citations
• Export to CSV, JSON, or Markdown format
• Works with multiple AI search platforms
• Real-time detection — waits for AI to finish responding before extraction
• 100% local processing — no data leaves your browser

**Supported Platforms:**
• Doubao (豆包)
• DeepSeek
• Perplexity (coming soon)
• Kimi (coming soon)
• Metaso (coming soon)

**Use Cases:**
• GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) analysis
• Competitor citation monitoring
• Content optimization research
• Academic research on AI search behavior

**Privacy First:**
CiteSnap processes everything locally. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any server. Fully open source on GitHub.

**How to Use:**
1. Visit a supported AI search engine and perform a search
2. Wait for the AI to finish responding
3. Click the CiteSnap icon in your toolbar
4. View extracted keywords and citations
5. Export with one click

## 详细描述（中文）

### CiteSnap - 一键提取 AI 搜索引用

AI 搜索引擎回答问题时会联网搜索并引用多篇文章，但这些引用链接无法直接批量复制。CiteSnap 帮你一键提取所有搜索关键词和引用链接，导出为结构化数据。

**核心功能：**
• 一键提取 AI 搜索的关键词和引用文章链接
• 多轮对话支持 — 每轮提问与引用一一配对
• 支持导出 CSV / JSON / Markdown 格式
• 多平台支持，持续扩展中
• 实时检测 — 等待 AI 回答完毕后自动提取
• 数据完全在本地处理，不上传任何信息

**已支持平台：**
• 豆包 (Doubao)
• DeepSeek
• Perplexity（即将支持）
• Kimi（即将支持）
• 秘塔搜索（即将支持）

**适用场景：**
• GEO（生成式引擎优化）数据分析
• 竞品在 AI 搜索中的曝光监控
• 内容优化策略研究
• AI 搜索引擎信息来源学术研究

**使用方法：**
1. 访问支持的 AI 搜索引擎，进行一次联网搜索
2. 等待 AI 回答完毕
3. 点击工具栏中的 CiteSnap 图标
4. 查看提取的关键词和引用链接
5. 一键导出数据
